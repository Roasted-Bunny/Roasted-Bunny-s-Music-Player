FAQ

  1. Why did you make this website? Couldn't you just use Soundcloud or YouTube to upload your songs?
    Yes, I could but... I want to learn HTML, and as I learn more the website will evolve. I'm hoping I can get a career out of this in the future as a web developer.
    Thank you to TheHopelessGamer and Omega for inspiring me to learn HTML.


  2. Do you monitor traffic?
    Yes I do, just out of curiosity.


  3. What website builder and host server did you use?
    None. My website is all handcoded by myself, apart from a few scripts.
    See Copyright Information for more information.
    As for the host server, I host my website myself on my Raspberry Pi.


  4. What does the old version of your website look like?
    You can take a look at the old version here.


  5. Do you make any money from this website?
    No, there are no advertisements on my website. This is just a fun project of mine.


  6. Do you store the files indefinitely for the custom uploaded songs?
    No, the files only run from your hard drive in your computer. It is only stored temporarily in your web browser in RAM.
